What a Simulation Log Is

When you run RTL simulation (e.g., with VCS, Xcelium, Questa), you typically see logs like:

Running test: add_test
RESULT: PASS
Cycles: 120

Running test: branch_test
RESULT: FAIL
Cycles: 180


Your job as frontend engineer:
-Extract results
-Summarize regression
-Identify failures
-Track performance
-Manual reading = waste of time.
-Automation = leverage.
